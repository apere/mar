Program Coordinator
SEED Academy @ MIT

As Program Coordinator for SEED Academy, I supported and fostered the learning of 90 secondary students grades 9-12. To this end, to ensure that best practices in teaching and student engagement are being exercised, I created and facilitated a professional development program for a teaching staff of 20. Additionally, I steered the development of pedagogy and curriculum toward an integrated, experiential and project based program model and foster relationships with educators, CBOs and community stakeholders in Cambridge, Lawrence and Boston.

Moreover, I developed successful data driven strategies for recruiting talented students from under-resourced and under-represented populations, supported both staff and student evaluations and fostered working relationships with staff, faculty, students and the greater MIT community.
