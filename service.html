Volunteer Reviewer February 2014
MIT Summer Research Program (MSRP)
Cambridge, Massachusetts

I reviewed applications of highly talented undergraduate applicants to the MIT Summer Research Program (MSRP) Qualified applicants are extended the opportunity to work in a world-class MIT research laboratory under the tutelage of experienced scientists and engineers. MSRP builds confidence and motivation in highly talented students from traditionally under represented groups and encourages them to pursue the Ph.D., toward the end of increasing representation of traditionally underrepresented scientists in research fields.

Volunteer Reviewer November 2013
CONVERGE Campus Preview Weekend MIT
Cambridge, Massachusetts

CONVERGE is an opportunity for prospective underrepresented graduate school applicants to come to campus, all expenses paid, and both meet with department representatives and learn how to best strengthen their applications to MIT. I reviewed a statement of purpose and conducted a one-on-one feedback session with a prospective MIT student to talk about his statement and answer any questions he had about the process.
